## LLMNR Poinsoning

The "NetBIOS and LLMNR Name Poisoning" attack is a man-in-the-middle technique that involves manipulating network name resolution protocols to redirect and intercept network traffic, enabling an attacker to compromise communication and gain unauthorized access to systems.
